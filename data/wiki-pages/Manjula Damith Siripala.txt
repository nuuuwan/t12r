Manjula Damith Siripala (also known as Manjula Damith and Manjula Siripala) is a former Sri Lankan rugby union player  domiciled in New Zealand.  


== Early childhood and Career ==
Manjula is educated at Nalanda College, Colombo. He also played for CH & FC in Sri Lanka  and Atkinson Rugby Club in Australia

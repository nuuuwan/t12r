Sodina police vidane, a well known traditional ayurvedic physician who lived in the kirindiwela “Parawatta” house, worked as the police vidane (Gammuladani) in the kirindiwela village in the siyane korale east in the gampaha district. He was born on 24.01.1894 at kirindiwela. Father- w.suwada veda he is also a talented traditional ayurvedic physician ( he is very good at curing social diseases ). Mother- Punchimali.

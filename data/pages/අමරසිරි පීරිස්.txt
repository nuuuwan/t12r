අමරසිරි පීරිස් is a Sri Lankan Musical artist who is well known as a Violinist and a Vocalist.


== පෙර අවධිය සහ ක්‍රියාන්විතය ==
අමරසිරි පීරිස් රේඩියෝ සිලෝන් හි නිවේදකයෙකු වූ ඇල්බට් පීරිස්ගේ පුතුයි. ඔහු මහරගම බුවනෙක විද්‍යාලයෙන් සහ University of Visual & Performing Arts (former Heywood Institute) අධ්‍යාපනය හැදෑරීය. ඔහු බැඳුනා SLBC as an A-grade violinist, and eventually, became a music conductor, a music controller and, at the time of my retirement, he worked as the Director, Music at SLBC. Peiris is an awarded Singer in Sri Lanka and he still performs on stage. His first popular song is Landune which was composed by maestro Premasiri Khemadasa. And then he sang many popular songs like
